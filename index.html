Services

We provide a unique opportunity for businesses around the world. Following the new "Open Data Marketplace" paradigm we seek to provide businesses and research facilities with high quality data about the auto-mobile industry directly sourced from drivers all around the world - completly free of charge!

Easy-to-use APIs

We provide our partners with two distinct API services. One API is designed to provide an easy way to query complex data from our database. The other API is designed to make it easy to upload data to our data base

Dashboard Service

We provide a modern dashboard to play around with our database. It can also be used to monitor your vehicle usage

Lot's of Data

We offer a lot of data that can be accessed and bundled however you like! You are interested in the number of Cars that drove 200km in Hololulu within the last 2 years? Our clever API will fetch you the data from our data base!

Open Data

The data we provide has been cleaned up and is standardized. That means that everyone can upload their data and access it - for free!

Our Mission

Open Data for a brighter future

OKAPI is a group with a mission: Provide free, easy to use data to all of our users to accelerate innovaton in the automotive industry. The world at large will greatly benefit from this project. But we can't do this alone - we need YOU to voluntarly upload your data to our database. Of course, you'll be rewarded by gaining access to said data base.
